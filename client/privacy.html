Privacy Policy

BountyScope is built for ethical hackers, researchers, penetration testers, and offensive security professionals. Protecting user privacy, scan confidentiality, research integrity, and platform security is a core part of our mission.

Information We Collect

BountyScope may collect account information, authentication data, scan metadata, endpoint analysis data, and vulnerability findings necessary to provide platform functionality, analytics, and security operations.

  • User account credentials
  • Scan targets submitted by users
  • Replay analysis metadata
  • Security research activity logs
  • Usage analytics for platform improvement
  • API request telemetry

Ethical Usage Requirements

BountyScope is designed strictly for authorized security testing, ethical hacking, bug bounty participation, and defensive research activities.

Users are solely responsible for ensuring they possess explicit authorization before testing systems, APIs, applications, infrastructure, or endpoints.

Data Protection

We implement security controls intended to help protect stored account information, authentication tokens, research activity, scan history, and operational platform data.

Sensitive vulnerability findings and offensive security research should always be handled responsibly and disclosed according to applicable laws, coordinated disclosure practices, and responsible disclosure standards.

Platform Monitoring

BountyScope may monitor platform usage, API traffic, automation behavior, scan activity, and system interactions to identify abuse, unauthorized usage, malicious automation, exploitation attempts, and attacks against the platform.

Third-Party Services

BountyScope may rely on third-party infrastructure, analytics providers, cloud platforms, authentication providers, and payment processors required to support core platform functionality.

Those services may process limited operational data required for authentication, analytics, infrastructure stability, and platform operations.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect platform changes, security improvements, operational requirements, legal obligations, or new platform functionality.

Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to Swarm Shield LLC.
